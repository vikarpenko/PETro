# PETro

A mini AR parrot you can play with and take care of.

## What it can do

- **Place & move** - tap any flat surface and Petro flies there.
- **Eats real food** - show it a pizza, banana, apple, or bowl and it swoops over to eat.
- **Pet it** - hold your hand over it for about a second and it reacts.
- **Needs attention** - ignore it too long and it dramatically drops dead.
- **Lives in your world** - hides behind real furniture and people.
- **Mimics your voice** - press the screen and say something; when you lift your finger, the parrot will repeat your words

## Built with

- **SwiftUI**
- **ARKit**
- **RealityKit**
- **Vision**
- **CoreML & YOLO**
- **AVFoundation**
